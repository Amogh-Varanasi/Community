Synopsis: “The United Forts of Pillowtown, headed by Abed, is at war with the Legit Republic of Blanketsburg, ruled by Troy. Annie sets up a hospital zone to treat casualties on both sides. Britta takes a stab at war-photography. Jeff tries to reunite two former friends turned mortal enemies using sarcasm.” (https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2127579/plotsummary?ref_=tt_ov_pl)

All of the themed Community episodes are great, but this one is the best. The Ken Burns documentary style adds drama and tension, as well as plenty of hilarious moments. The war between The United Forts of Pillowtown and the Legit Republic of Blanketsburg help the audience understand just how important Abed and Troy are to each other and the rest of the study group. It’s a great episode that you should definitely watch!
